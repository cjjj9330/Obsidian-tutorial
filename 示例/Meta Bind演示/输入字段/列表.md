### 列表

```meta-bind
INPUT[list(showcase):list]
```

`VIEW[{list}][link]`

```meta-bind
INPUT[list(showcase, multiLine):list]
```

### 列表建议器

```meta-bind
INPUT[listSuggester(optionQuery(#example-note), showcase):list2]
```

`VIEW[{list2}][link]`

```meta-bind
INPUT[listSuggester(optionQuery(#example-note), useLinks(false), showcase):list3]
```
